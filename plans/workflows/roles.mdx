Ref Plans is flexible and built for teams. How you use it depends on your role.

  <Card title="Senior / Staff Engineer" icon="layer-group" href="/plans/workflows/role-senior-staff">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Vibecoder" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/plans/workflows/role-vibecoder">
    Scale context beyond single sessions. Maintain cohesive context across large projects.
