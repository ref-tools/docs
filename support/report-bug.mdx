## Gather Details

- Collect the tool call transcript.
- Note the repo or resource involved.
- Capture timestamps and credit IDs.

## Submit

- Email `hello@ref.tools` with logs attached.
- Tag bugs in the dashboard feedback form when available.
- Include screenshots if the UI behaved unexpectedly.

## Follow Up

- Expect a response within one business day.
- Urgent outages escalate to the on-call engineer.
- Track status in your support ticket history.
